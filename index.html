My Businesses
Burton Automotive was the first of my businesses which focused on basic retail car sales. More than anything it seemed like a fun way to get me through college and a way to acquire some entrepreneurial experience. What started as a "side hustle" or hobby quickly developed into a full scale business, however. As with all businesses, pivoting was necessary to stay profitable. I quickly discovered the wholesale side of selling cars--selling through auctions. Selling cars at auctions is a much quicker and smooth process, however the downside is that cars sell for slightly less than retail prices. Despite this, I quickly learned that, with volume and buying the right cars, I could greatly streamline my business and increase profits.
Carbitrage Investments was created as a sister company to Burton Automotive. Carbitrage Investments was named as a play on words combinding "cars" with the word "arbitrage". It was created as a way to take advantage of the variance in wholesale car prices. Through algorithms and programmed searches, cars being sold even below wholesale value can easily be found and purchased. The convenience factor lies in the fact that the cars in question are already at one of the many nationwide auctions which allows them to be sold for their true wholesale values in an extremely fast, low friction transaction.
